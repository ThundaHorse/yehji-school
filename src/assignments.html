Detailed Assignments

Module Discussions (30 points)

Participate actively in ongoing discussions (Modules 2, 4, & 6). You can choose between two options:

  • Option 1: Discussion Forum
    • Initial Response: 400–600 words (or video/audio). Due the first Sunday of the module window.
    • Replies: At least two thoughtful replies to peers. Due the final Sunday of the module window.
  • Option 2: Virtual Group Discussion
    • Group Meeting: Convene on Zoom (30-60 mins) to discuss prompts. Record the session. Post link by first Sunday.
    • Follow-Up Post: Individual summary (400-600 words) posted by the final Sunday.
Requirement: Citations and reference lists (APA 7th ed.) are mandatory for all posts.

Key Assessment: Literacy Case Study (160 points)

A balanced, holistic approach to assessment and instruction for a P–12 student experiencing reading/writing difficulties. The project is completed in segments:

  1. Student Literacy Biography (10%): Detailed picture of the student as a reader/writer.
  2. Assessment Results (5%): Pretest/Posttest chart and analysis of 6 literacy domains.
  3. Intervention Plan (10%): 3 SMART goals (2 reading, 1 writing) with research-based rationale.
  4. Recorded Tutoring Session (20%): Video of one full session and a reflection on teaching.
  5. Recommendations (10%): For parents (layman's terms) and educators (professional terms).
  6. Overall Reflection (10%): Synthesis of the entire tutoring process.
  7. References & Appendices (5% & 25%): APA references, Permission Forms, Surveys, 4 Tutoring Plans, 4 Student Work Samples.
